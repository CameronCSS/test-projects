## About

Valentines day project that shows a happy little bunny who asks 'Will you be mine?' 
<br>
When you try to click ```No``` the button runs away for the cursor and the bunny begins to cry.
<br>
When you finally click ```Yes``` a .gif of the bunny dancing and celebrating is played.
